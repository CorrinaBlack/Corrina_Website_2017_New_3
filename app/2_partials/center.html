Windows Phone Dev Center for learning

Windows Phone Dev Center is the go to place for documentation to
assist with learning about the capabilities of Windows Phone and
the inner workings of the platform as well as tools to
build and manage app deployment, updates, and
monetization.


My role was Principal Research and Design Lead. My
team and I worked on several iterations of the site.
Initially, our focus was research and definition of an
information architecture appropriate for users. We
then designed navigation and content patterns as
well as style guides to facilitate work with four content
and development teams. My core responsibilities included
research, interaction design, visual design, motion design,
information design, prototyping, and art direction.
