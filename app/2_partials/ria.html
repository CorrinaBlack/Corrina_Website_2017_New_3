Silverlight is a platform for creating RIA apps

Silverlight began as an application development platform for writing
and running rich internet applications (RIAs). It focused on
multimedia, graphics, animations, and streaming media.
Microsoft invested in Silverlight to build a presence in the
RIA space... a space dominated by Adobe Flash and Flex.


My role was Senior Research and Design Lead. My
team and I defined the target developer persona,
designed aspects of the consumer experience, and
designed the developer experience from core
controls, to control themes, to sample apps, and
rapid application development tools in both
Visual Studio and Expression Blend. My core
responsibilities included research, interaction design, visual
design, motion design, prototyping, and art direction.
