Microsoft Accelerator for entrepreneurs

The Microsoft Accelerator program is an initiative to empower
entrepreneurs. The focus is on later-stage startups, and
to provide the tools, resources, connections,
knowledge, and expertise needed to build
successful companies.


My role was Principal Design Lead. I
convinced leadership to invest in design
education and support for the program,
and, then, organized a team of experts from
inside and outside of Microsoft to drive the design
agenda for the initial Seattle cohort. My team and I also
defined a program that was used in subsequent cohorts. My
core responsibilities included storytelling, program definition,
interaction design, visual design, and art direction.
